Nutella Ice Cream

Here is a recipe for a homemade ice cream.Its so delicious and can make in a short time. Feel free to experiment with it.

Ingredients

  • Heavy whipping cream – 473 ml (Around 2 cups), chilled
  • Vanilla extract – 1 tsp
  • Sweetened condensed milk – 1 can (14 oz), chilled
  • Nutella – 1/2 cup
  • Chopped almonds, mini chocolate chipsor marshmallows – 1/4 cup each (optional)

Method

  1. Combine condensed milk and nutella in a bowl. Whisk until smooth. Set aside until ready to use.
  2. In a large bowl, combine heavy whipping cream and vanilla extract. Using an electric hand mixer at medium-high speed, beat for about 5 – 6 minutes until medium peaks form. Add nutella and continue to beat for 20-30 seconds, scraping in between. Stir in the chopped almonds, mini chocolate chips or marshmallows, if using. Transfer to a freezer-safe container and freeze for 5 – 6 hrs or until firm. Enjoy! overnight.
